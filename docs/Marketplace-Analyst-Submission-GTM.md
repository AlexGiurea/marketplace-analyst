# Marketplace Analyst — Submission GTM

Marketplace Analyst is not a chatbot that plays Marketplace Simulation for the student, but an analyst layered onto the student's own run that makes each quarter easier to understand by translating what the numbers and components mean, connecting decisions to outcomes, surfacing checkable facts, figures, and calculations, and helping teams move with more clarity, stronger reasoning, and better evidence without ever taking ownership of the strategy away from them.

It should be positioned as a clarity-and-evidence partner for Marketplace Simulation: a tool that helps students interpret performance, understand tradeoffs, check the math that matters, and defend their choices with confidence, while making no claim that it solves the simulation, invents results, or replaces student judgment or authorship.

**One-line version:** Marketplace Analyst helps students interpret their Marketplace Simulation run, check the math that matters, and make better-supported decisions without outsourcing the strategy.
